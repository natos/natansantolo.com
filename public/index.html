Hi there!

Nice to meet you, my name is Natan Santolo. I'm the founder of Three Leaves and Technical Advisor at Scalabl.

Currently I'm in Amsterdam, at Spaaza collaborating to make the best customer experience possible for their platform.

Before in New York, I co-founded first Tiplinks and then Caliber. And way before in Buenos Aires, I created Chico UI for MercadoLibre, I taught web development in Escuela Da Vinci and founded the first user experience team in Cubika.

I've been designing websites and apps for fun and profit since 1998. And I feel I'm still fascinated like the first day. When I have spare time, I like to collaborate coding in different projects specially html, css and javascript, I occasionally tweet about web development and other boring stuff. I also enjoy writing nonsenses in a little blog and I like to collect and share music.
